Basics of Training

Dog training is a very useful tool. You can make money training other dogs, or you can just train your own dog to be a great companion. The training itself takes alot of time and effort, but it is very rewarding. Common misconceptions are that teaching tricks is the main thing. This isn't at all the case. Your dog knowing how to shake hands won't help much at all when he or she is about to run in the middle of the road. Rather in this situation, a common come back command would work much better than any trick ever would.

Commands to Teach

As mentioned above, tricks are far less important than obedience commands. The commands that should be taught to a dog are as follows: come, stay, drop it, no, place, and off. These commands will be essential to your dog, and in some situations like above it could save your dogs life!
